## High level overview

This week, the Mithril team completed the refactoring of the non-recursive SNARK proof to not carry the circuit verification key, the upgrade of the `midnight-zk` library to `2.3.3`, the enablement of the Plutus friendly hash for the IVC proving, and the refactoring of the error handling from the Midnight library. They continued work on analyzing and optimizing the testing strategy of the STM SNARK modules.

The team also continued work on implementing the aggregator CLI commands for the configuration parameters and on creating the runbook for the on-chain configuration parameters. They completed the simplification of the `SignersRegistrationRetriever` trait and continued work on shipping the Mithril signer node binary in the Cardano node bundle, the release of the `2628` distribution, the upgrade to Cardano `11.1`, and the update of the DMQ node to `0.7.0.0`.

Finally, the team completed the transfer of the repository to the `IntersectMBO` organization, and worked on the preparation of the documentation website for Docusaurus `v4`.
