## High level overview

This week, the Mithril team completed the prover input preparation and the SNARK proof verification of the recursive SNARK aggregation primitives, as well as the adaptation of the certificate chain to support the recursive SNARK. They continued work on creating the SNARK proof with the circuit, wiring the SNARK proof in the aggregate signature, refactoring the preparation of the prover input, adding the missing ancillary data for the IVC aggregate signature creation, and updating the Midnight ZK library audit status for SNARK.

Additionally, they completed the optimization of the end-to-end tests for SNARK, the update of the ledger state snapshot selection in artifacts, the upgrade to Pallas `1.1.0`, and the enhancement of the support for discontinued signed entity types in the aggregator.

Finally, the team continued work on shipping the Mithril signer node binary in the Cardano node bundle, the release of the `2625` distribution, and the fix for the aggregator switch in the explorer.
