## Overview of sprints [110][sp-110], [111][sp-111], [112][sp-112] and [113][sp-113].

### Summary

#### Leios

The [mux egress prioritisation][issue#5262] issue was resolved, delivering static
priority-based scheduling for mini-protocols.  Work on
[weighted fair queueing with token bucket support][pr#5322] was concluded: it allows
bursty protocols to send multiple SDUs back-to-back without starving other
mini-protocols.  However, right now we don't have evidence that the additional
complexity is really needed to achive better performance.  A [TxSubmission V2
demo][pr#5354] is being merged, and an alternative design [without a central
decision thread][pr#5352] is being explored.

#### Ouroboros-Network

Tx-submission v2 now [returns results from `submitTxToMempool`][pr#5357], a
prerequisite for DMQ-Node integration.  The long-standing [ChurnMode
refactoring][pr#4962] was merged, together with a [property-based tx integrity
test][pr#5356] for `cardano-diffusion`.  Nodes now [warn about misconfigured
local socket permissions][pr#5363] (an issue identified by Claude LLM).  A [fix
for initial syncing targets][pr#5362] is under review, which will reduce load
on bootstrap relays during node startup by
setting lower active-peer targets before the first churn cycle.

#### DMQ-Node

Versions [0.3.0.0][pr#43] and [0.4.0.0][pr#45] were released.  The 0.4.0.0
release includes [trace-dispatcher integration][pr#44], [Prometheus
metrics][pr#51], and [CLI improvements][pr#47].  Work is ongoing on an
[announciness metric for peer selection][pr#53] — scoring peers by how often
they are first to announce a valid signature — and on [connecting the DMQ kernel
to `cardano-node`][pr#35].
