## High level summary

The SRE team continues work on Cardano environment improvements and general maintenance.

Some notable recent changes, updates or improvements include:

* For this biweekly period, SRE has been heavily engaged in support activity
  for the new USDCx service, now publicly announced [here](https://www.iog.io/news/usdcx-on-cardano-is-live-a-new-chapter-for-stablecoin-utility).

* The Dijkstra network was respun to facilitate new genesis parameter optimizations.
