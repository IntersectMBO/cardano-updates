## High level summary

The SRE team continues work on Cardano environment improvements and general maintenance.

Some notable recent changes, updates or improvements include:

* The IOG private mainnet pools were retired this week.  The
  [IOG1](https://adastat.net/pools/d9812f8d30b5db4b03e5b76cfd242db9cd2763da4671ed062be808a0)
  public stakepool remains active and forging.

* An internal Dijkstra network was spun up for testing of the upcoming
intra-era hard fork, followed by Dijkstra hark fork testing.
