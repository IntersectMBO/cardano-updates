## High level overview

This week, the Mithril team completed the full review of the recursive SNARK circuit prototype, the impact assessment of SNARK on Mithril protocol security, and the support for multiple proof systems in the STM library. They also completed the SNARK proof generation and verification in end to end tests. The team started work on the recursive SNARK circuit, including creating a new module in the STM library, preparing a testing plan for the IVC circuit, preparing production readiness for circuit keys and trusted setup, and supporting test mode for the recursive circuit. They also continued work on refactoring the bytes codec in the STM library.

Additionally, they completed the Cardano blocks and transactions client CLI implementation, and continued progressing on partial block range support, the security parameter offset signing, and the explorer update for Cardano blocks and transactions.

Finally, the team completed the upgrade of `reqwest` to `0.13`, and continued work on the upgrade to Cardano `10.7` and the DMQ node update to `0.4.1.0`.
