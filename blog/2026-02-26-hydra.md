### What did the team achieve?

* Fixed a race-condition bug on decommits [#2500](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/2500)
* Fixed bugs/stuck node issue around commits [#2491](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/pull/2491)
* Resolved issues users were having around computing time in contracts on the hydra-node [#2162](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/2162)
* Fixed bug around deposits and chain rollbacks [#2488](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/2488)
* Improve error reporting for missing script witness [#2507](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/2507)
* Fix infinite AckSn loop bug related to decommits/commits [#2510](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/pull/2510)
* Add a few additional logging outputs aroud chain sync status [#2483](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/2483), [#2393](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/2393)
* Great progress on partial fanout [#1667](https://github.com/cardano-scaling/hydra/issues/1667)
